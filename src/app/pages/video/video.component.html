About this Course

Think about an Item that makes you feel happy and close to God. It could be something you see, hear, smell, taste, or touch. This thing should be special to you and help you feel close to God. For example, some people might feel connected to God when they see a beautiful sunset, while others might feel close to God when they hug a loved one. Whatever your special thing is, it should remind you of who God created you to be and help you focus on your true identity. This can be a helpful way to feel more connected to God and remember how much He loves you. In this part, you will select an item that best shows your personality.

Learning Outcomes

Discover your special object: Find the special things that make you special: Everyone is special in their own way. The things that make you special are just for you and show people how you are different. Some things go well with your personality. Your goal is to find those things that show who you are.
Make yourself better: Learn how using symbols can help you understand who you are. The things you choose can show people your personality and give them a better understanding of you. You can also learn more about yourself and how others see you by picking the right things. This can help you be a better person and help others see you better.
Feel connected to your special object: Think about your feelings when you are with your special thing. It should make you feel happy and close to God. Your special thing helps you tell the story of your life. You can also use it to imagine what you want your future to be like.

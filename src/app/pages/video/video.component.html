Welcome to This course

Think about an Item that makes you feel happy and close to God. It could be something you see, hear, smell, taste, or touch. This thing should be special to you and help you feel close to God. For example, some people might feel connected to God when they see a beautiful sunset, while others might feel close to God when they hug a loved one. Whatever your special thing is, it should remind you of who God created you to be and help you focus on your true identity. This can be a helpful way to feel more connected to God and remember how much He loves you. In this part, you will select an item that best shows your personality. Posted - 2 Months ago
